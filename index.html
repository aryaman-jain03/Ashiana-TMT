Backed by the legacy of KSAL, Ashiana® TMT represents more than just reinforcement steel — it represents the trust of thousands of families, contractors, and builders who refuse to compromise when it comes to the structures they call home.

Ashiana® TMT Fe550 SD is engineered to deliver superior strength, outstanding ductility, and long-lasting durability across modern construction requirements. Every bar that leaves our facility carries the promise of a brand that has spent over two decades refining its craft. We don't just manufacture steel — we manufacture confidence.

Key Features

Engineered for Performance. Built for the Long Haul.

Six defining properties — precision-manufactured into every single bar we produce. These aren't marketing promises. They're measurable realities, tested and verified at every stage of production.
01

Superior
Strength

Ashiana® TMT Fe550 SD bars deliver a minimum yield strength of 550 MPa — comfortably exceeding the BIS IS:1786 standard. Less waste, more strength. That's smart construction.

02

Consistent
Quality

Every Ashiana® TMT coil is rolled to precise dimensional tolerances, tested in-house, and cross-checked against BIS norms. Batch after batch, you get the same reliable product. No surprises. No shortcuts.

03

Corrosion
Resistant

Ashiana® TMT bars are produced with a controlled carbon-sulphur-phosphorus chemistry that significantly reduces corrosion risk. Structures last longer, maintenance costs drop, and your investment holds its value over decades.

04

Excellent
Weldability

Ashiana® TMT bars have a controlled Carbon Equivalent (CE) value that makes them highly weldable — reducing the risk of heat-affected zone cracking during site fabrication. On a busy construction site, that kind of dependability is priceless.

05

Superior
Ductility (SD Grade)

The 'SD' in Fe550 SD stands for Special Ductility. Our bars have a high Uniform Elongation (UE) and a favourable Ultimate-to-Yield ratio — the preferred choice in seismic zones and earthquake-prone regions.

06

Earthquake
Resistant

Ashiana® TMT Fe550 SD bars are designed to perform under dynamic loads, meeting and exceeding IS:13920 requirements for ductile detailing. When you build with Ashiana® TMT, you're building a structure that fights back.

Certifications

Built to the Highest
Standards. Certified to
Prove It.

Certifications aren't just badges on a brochure. They're the result of rigorous third-party verification — external auditors walking through our plant, confirming that what we claim matches what we deliver.

BIS Certification
IS:1786 Fe550 SD

The Bureau of Indian Standards IS:1786 is the definitive standard for high-strength deformed steel bars in India. Fe550 SD is the highest-ductility grade. Our BIS mark is not self-declared — it is granted and periodically renewed based on independent audits of our manufacturing process and finished product.

IS:13920 — Ductile
Detailing Standard

This standard governs how steel behaves in earthquake-resistant concrete structures. Ashiana® TMT Fe550 SD's high Uniform Elongation and controlled yield-to-ultimate ratio make it fully compliant with IS:13920 — giving structural engineers the confidence to specify our bars in seismic design without reservation.

In-House
Laboratory Testing

Before a single bar leaves our facility, it goes through a battery of tests — tensile testing, bend and re-bend tests, chemical composition analysis, and dimensional checks. Our in-house lab operates to NABL-aligned protocols, ensuring the numbers on our test certificates reflect the actual product you receive on site.
